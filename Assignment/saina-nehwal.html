Saina Nehwal

Saina Nehwal is a pioneering figure in Indian badminton, known for her remarkable achievements on the international stage. Born on March 17, 1990, in Haryana, India, she became a national sports icon by becoming the first Indian woman to win an Olympic medal in badminton at the 2012 London Olympics. Over the years, Saina has amassed numerous titles, establishing herself as one of the sport's all-time greats.

Her journey from a small town to becoming the World No. 1 in 2015 is nothing short of inspirational. Saina has won over 24 international titles, including 11 Superseries titles, and has consistently represented India at various global events. Her resilience and ability to overcome challenges have made her a role model for aspiring athletes across the world.

Key Achievements

2012 London Olympics

First Indian woman to win an Olympic medal in badminton - Bronze

2015 World No. 1

Became the first Indian woman to achieve World No. 1 ranking

Commonwealth Games

Gold medals in 2010 (Delhi) and 2018 (Gold Coast)
