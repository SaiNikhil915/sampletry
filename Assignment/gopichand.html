A Badminton Legend

Pullela Gopichand is a former All England champion and the current chief national coach of the Indian badminton team. His coaching career has been defined by mentoring world-class players such as Saina Nehwal and PV Sindhu, leading them to major international victories.

Born in 1973, Gopichand pursued badminton passionately and achieved the pinnacle of success when he won the All England Open Badminton Championships in 2001. Following his retirement, he founded the Gopichand Badminton Academy, which has been at the forefront of producing top badminton talent in India.
