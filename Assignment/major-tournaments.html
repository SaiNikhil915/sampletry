Top International Tournaments

Indian players have competed in prestigious tournaments such as the All England Open, World Championships, and the Olympics.

Some of the major achievements include PV Sindhu’s medals in the World Championships and Saina Nehwal’s bronze in the London Olympics.

All England Open

One of the oldest and most prestigious badminton tournaments in the world.

World Badminton Championships

The premier event for badminton players, held every two years.

Summer Olympics

Featuring badminton as an official sport since 1992, showcasing the best players globally.

Badminton Asia Championships

A key tournament that features top players from across Asia.

Thomas Cup

The premier men's team championship in badminton.

Uber Cup

The women's equivalent of the Thomas Cup, showcasing top female players.

Malaysia Open

A popular Super 750 event on the BWF circuit.

Indonesia Open

Another prestigious Super 1000 event, known for its high competition level.

China Open

A major tournament that attracts top players from around the world.

Singapore Open

A prominent tournament that draws many international competitors.
