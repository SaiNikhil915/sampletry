PV Sindhu

PV Sindhu is one of the most celebrated athletes in Indian badminton, known for her consistency, strength, and remarkable ability to win on the biggest stages. Born on July 5, 1995, in Hyderabad, India, she became the first Indian woman to win a silver medal at the Olympics in badminton during the 2016 Rio Olympics. She followed up this achievement with a bronze at the 2020 Tokyo Olympics.

Sindhu's list of achievements is long and impressive. She has won medals in almost every major international tournament, including the World Championships, Commonwealth Games, and Asian Games.
